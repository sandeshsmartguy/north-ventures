Editorial at Aranca provides clients with three levels of edits: Standard Intensive Express

Standard editing involves a light-touch review of the content to check for the basics such as flow, consistency, grammar, punctuation and spelling. Minimalistic changes are made to the wording of the content, and any language-related errors are fixed at this stage.
Intensive editing, also known as comprehensive editing, is where the editor verifies the technical correctness of the content. More importantly, fact checking is done at this stage, and content is re-structured and re-organized, if required, while retaining the intended meaning. The editor looks for logic and flow of thought and makes the copy concise to enhance readability. They also check whether consistency has been maintained in style and format. The editor, based on the core theme of the copy, revises headlines and captions as needed.

Skill-Set Measurement, Brain Mapping & Mentorship


MIE Experts have observed that whichever industry a person works for; the personal traits / personality doesn’t change much and remains constant throughout the life. A person working in a job profile that mismatches his personality will ultimately face issues such as job frustration or lack of job satisfaction, low work motivation and poor performance leading to a below average career progress.

MIE will offer special services of skill-set measurement and brain mapping through advanced techniques like DMIT (Finger-Print Scanning), Qualitative Interviews, Psychological tests, and Psycho-analytical test among others. These techniques help in measuring inclination of the brain, core area of interest and attitudinal bend towards a particular task to be done in long term. The gathered information is then used for identifying favourable career options for students. We conduct skillset measurement and brain mapping around 4/5 times over the course of 2 years and share the results with students, allowing them to understand their strengths and weaknesses and accordingly focus on enhancing their skillsets.

Advance Training –Soft Skills and Hard Skills


MBA / PGDM courses in India provide an overall management perspective and grooms students to become a future manager / entrepreneur. However there exists a gap between modern day career choices available and the education system.

For E.g.: If a Student is strong in statistics and wants to pursue a career in business analytics or if a student is good in social media promotion and wants to pursue a career in digital marketing; he will have to undergo a management program in Marketing in which Business Analytics /Digital Marketing will just be a subject. This does not give students proper insights about the industry and the job they want to take up. Neither in this process, have they acquired skillsets to do so.

MIE plans to provide hard skill training requires for making a student industry-ready. Training will be conducted on subjects like:

  •   Excel & Advance Excel
  •   SPSS, SAS, R programming
  •   HR Analytics
  •   Six Sigma certification programs
  •   Digital Marketing (Servicing/ Planning and Strategy formulation)
  •   SEO/SEM/Google Analytics
  •   Financial courses Share trading, News analysis and more
